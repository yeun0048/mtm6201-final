Membership

Home / Membership

Join the Wave Recreation Center and dive into a world of health, fun, and community! Our membership gives you access to a wide range of facilities and programs designed for all ages and fitness levels. Enjoy our state-of-the-art gym, swimming pools, sports courts, group fitness classes, and recreational programs. Members also receive exclusive discounts on special events, workshops, and personal training sessions.

Policies

At Wave Recreation Center, we strive to provide a safe, welcoming, and enjoyable environment for all members. Memberships are available in flexible plans—monthly, annual, or family—with cancellations requiring 30 days’ notice. Members are expected to follow our code of conduct, including showing respect to staff and fellow members, wearing proper athletic attire, and using equipment safely. Children under 12 must be supervised at all times, and personal belongings should be stored in provided lockers, as the center is not responsible for lost or stolen items. Fees for classes, workshops, and events are generally non-refundable, except when the center cancels an activity or in special circumstances. By joining, members agree to these policies, helping us maintain a friendly and secure community for everyone.
